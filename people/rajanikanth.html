Biographical Sketch

Rajanikanth N Kashi is currently working at Honeywell Technology Solutions, Bangalore, in the Communications, Navigation, and Surveillance COE. He is currently working on the Integrated Multi Mode Receiver project as a Principal systems Engineer. At Honeywell he has been involved with both Systems and Software development of the Integrated Navigation Receiver and the Integrated Aircraft Environment and Surveillance Systems. Rajanikanth started his career with the Bharat Electronics in Military Communication Group where he worked on V/UHF Transreceiver systems and GPS. After a brief stint with Wipro Systems working on the Asynchronous Transfer Mode (AT&T GTE Communication Systems project), Rajanikanth moved to Aeronautical Development Agency (ADA), Bangalore, where he worked as a Design Team Leader for the LCA Mission software and conceived ARSENAL â€“ An airborne multimode radar simulator/framework, using design patterns.

He holds a BE (Electronics) from the BMS College of Engineering,Bangalore, and MSc (Engg), in Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. He is a INCOSE Certified Systems Engineering Professional and is a member of the Aeronautical society of India, Computer Society, and the Institiute of Navigation.

Rajanikanth holds two granted US patents, an european patent and a fourth US patent which is currently filed.
